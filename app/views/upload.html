Batch-upload is available to upload information related to all relevant samples in the same spreadsheet.

Metadata ensures that our data is stored according to our standards. In order to user our uploader you need to download an excel sheet that you must fill out with the proper information.

Remember to check the tab ‘Attribute description’ indicating the required contents. In the template, each line corresponds to one sample.

We require you to upload information in a specific format. In order to be flexible and allow to include your own columns, you can use the field titled ‘Notes‘ to add separated fields that later you can filter

(e.g. "doctor;laboratory;batch_name...")
